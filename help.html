Help & Support

We’re here to assist you — Chariot Technologies Uganda

1. Need Assistance?

Our support team is available to help riders and drivers resolve issues quickly. Whether you need help with your account, trips, payments, or app features, we’re ready to assist.

2. Common Help Topics

Account Issues

  • Account not logging in
  • Phone number changes
  • Profile update problems

Ride Issues

  • Lost items
  • Incorrect charges
  • Trip did not arrive
  • Driver or rider misconduct

Driver Help

  • Document approval delays
  • Payout issues
  • Receiving fewer trip requests

3. Lost & Found

If you lost an item during a trip, contact the driver directly through the trip history page in the app. If you're unable to reach the driver, reach out to our support team for assistance.

4. Complaints & Reporting

To report a trip issue, unsafe driving, or inappropriate behavior, use the in-app report feature or email our support team. All reports are taken seriously and reviewed by our safety team.
