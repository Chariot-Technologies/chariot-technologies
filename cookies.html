Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Chariot Technologies uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when visiting our website or mobile application.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your device that help us improve site performance, remember preferences, and analyze usage.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Authentication and login
  • Analytics and performance tracking
  • Saving user preferences
  • Personalizing content

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use services such as Google Analytics, which place their own cookies for analytics and personalized advertising.

4. Managing Cookies

You may block, delete, or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page.
