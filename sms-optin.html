SMS Program Overview

Chariot is a mobility and ride-hailing platform. This campaign sends: (1) One-Time Passwords (OTP) for account verification (sign-up, sign-in, password reset) and (2) transactional notifications directly related to user-initiated activity (ride requested, driver assigned/arrived, trip started/ended, payment/receipt updates). No marketing or promotional content is sent.

Message Flow (User-Initiated Only)

Messages are sent only after a user action in the Chariot apps (e.g., tap “Log in / Sign up” to request OTP, request a ride, receive trip status updates, and payment/receipt notifications after trip completion).

How users opt in (in-app)

Users provide their mobile number and consent in the Chariot app during sign-up, sign-in, or when requesting an OTP. The opt-in disclosure is shown at the point of phone number collection and states:

Opt-in disclosure shown in app:
“By continuing, you agree to receive SMS from Chariot for account verification (OTP) and transactional notifications related to your account/ride activity. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Privacy Policy: https://chariot.ug/privacy.html”
